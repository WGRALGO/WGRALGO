# Richard “Rich” BlackMan

I’m Richard “Rich” BlackMan, President & Founder of The Wealth Gap Resolution Algorithm™ Inc., a nonprofit organization focused on turning knowledge into generational wealth.

Our website is completely free for anybody to use, with zero ads, zero tracking cookies, and no paywalls.

I create free educational resources, open-source software guides, empowerment tools, and learning games focused on financial literacy, digital safety, scam prevention, AI literacy, workforce skills, and entrepreneurship.

I am not a coder, but I believe open-source software can be one of the most powerful tools for public empowerment. That is why I created the Empowerment Hub, a section of our website with several webpages dedicated to open-source software and free learning resources people can use to learn, build, organize, protect themselves, and grow.

I have also created educational games that are being turned into Android APKs. The goal is to make them available to the public as free and open-source apps.

Knowledge is the weapon. Open source is one way we share it.
